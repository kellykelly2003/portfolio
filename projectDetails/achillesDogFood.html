Achilles Organic Dog Food

The Details

This is a fully functioning site designed in WordPress. The customer requested pre-selected color palettes to use. Achilles Organic Dog Food is made up of many areas. Social media links are located in several strategic sites to be visible to visitors. There is a blog area, where the owner wanted to communicate directly with her followers. This area contained a lot of diy tips to encourage dog owners who don't want to purchase their organic food, make their own. The shop section includes several products with prices and sale items. The customer is able to create their own account or purchase as a guest.
